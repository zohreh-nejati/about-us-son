How it started

Our dream is global learning transformation

Our journey began with a simple idea: make Dribble more accessible and meaningful. Back in 1989, a group of passionate individuals came together with a shared vision — to solve a specific problem that people faced every day. We started small, working late nights from kitchens, garages, and local coffee shops.

Our vision

Empowering Lives Though Education

Education is more than just a pathway to employment—it's a powerful tool for personal growth, social mobility, and long-term change. By providing access to quality learning opportunities, we enable individuals to unlock their potential, build confidence, and shape a better future for themselves and their communities.

Our mission

Learning For All Everywhere

Education should know no boundaries. Whether in classrooms, homes, or remote villages, everyone deserves the chance to learn, grow, and succeed. “Learning for All, Everywhere” is our commitment to making education accessible, inclusive, and adaptable—regardless of location, age, or background.
